Coriander Fried Chicken

This fried chicken that uses the aroma and delicacy of coriander is indeed interesting to try. This recipe can be followed by anyone, including people who are just learning to cook.

Ingredients :

  • 1 chicken, add salt and lime, wash and set aside
  • 1/2 lime, squeezed
  • 1 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 4 red onions
  • 8 pieces of garlic
  • 3 tablespoons coriander grains
  • 1 tsp pepper powder
  • 1 piece of ginger

How to make :

  1. Marinate the chicken with ground spices plus soy sauce, sweet soy sauce, sugar, salt, and lime juice. Leave it for at least 30 minutes for the flavors to infuse.
  2. Fry until cooked (medium heat).
